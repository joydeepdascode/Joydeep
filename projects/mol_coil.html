Advances in Plasma Confinement Studies

Our research delves into improving plasma stability within the magnetic confinement devices by leveraging high-fidelity simulations and machine learning models.

Research Objective

The goal is to analyze the magnetic confinement efficiency and devise optimal methods for improving stability within fusion reactors like DIII-D and Aditya-U.

Methodologies Employed

We implemented advanced Particle-in-Cell (PIC) models coupled with reinforcement learning to study the particle dynamics under varying conditions.

Results & Findings

Conclusion and Future Directions

Our findings provide a new perspective on plasma confinement techniques, paving the way for further experimental validation and integration into reactor designs.
