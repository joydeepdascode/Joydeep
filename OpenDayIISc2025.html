Introduction

Welcome to PlasmaExplorer

PlasmaExplorer is a basic visualization tool designed to illustrate simplified plasma behavior under different conditions. By adjusting parameters such as magnetic field strength (B), wavenumber (k), twist factor, and number of CPU cores, users can observe how these factors influence plasma dynamics in an idealized setting.

In real-life scenarios, plasma behavior is far more complex and chaotic than what is represented here. Factors such as turbulence, instabilities, and nonlinear interactions play a significant role in shaping particle motion, making real plasma systems highly unpredictable and computationally intensive to model accurately.

This tool serves as an introductory illustration, providing a simplified glimpse into how magnetic fields influence particle movement. While it does not capture the full complexity of plasma physics, it offers a starting point for understanding fundamental concepts.
